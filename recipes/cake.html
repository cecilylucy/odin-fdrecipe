Description

Vanilla cake is a soft and fluffy dessert that is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or enjoying with a cup of tea. It is easy to prepare and can be decorated with frosting or served plain.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients and milk, mixing until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased baking tin.
  7. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool before serving or decorating.
