Description

Pilau is a fragrant rice dish cooked with spices, onions, and meat or vegetables. It is a favorite meal during family gatherings and celebrations because of its rich flavor and delicious aroma.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 500g beef, cut into cubes
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons pilau masala
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
