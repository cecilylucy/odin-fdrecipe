Pilau

Home

Description

Pilau is a fragrant rice dish cooked with spices, onions, and meat or vegetables. It is a favorite meal during family gatherings and celebrations because of its rich flavor and delicious aroma.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 500g beef, cut into cubes
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons pilau masala
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil and fry the onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the pilau masala and tomatoes.
  4. Add the rice and mix well with the spices.
  5. Pour in the beef stock and season with salt.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is tender.
  7. Serve hot with salad or kachumbari.
