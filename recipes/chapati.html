Description

Chapati is a soft, round flatbread that is popular in many East African and South Asian countries. It is made from wheat flour, water, salt, and a little oil, making it a simple but delicious accompaniment to stews, vegetables, or tea.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • Extra flour for rolling

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the oil and warm water, then knead into a smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into equal balls and roll each into a thin circle.
  5. Cook each chapati on a hot pan until both sides are golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with your favorite stew or vegetables.
